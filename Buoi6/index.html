Sword art online : IF

Sword art online : Integral Factor(ソードアート・オンライン インテグラル・ファクター?) is an Android and iOS freemium game, announced during Tokyo Game Show 2017 on September 23, 2017 and was released globally on March 26, 2018.[citation needed] The game is an Online RPG. The game takes place in Sword Art Online, where players can use their own avatar to clear the game, alongside a partner named Koharu (コハル). The game will allow players to create their own story of clearing Aincrad from the start of the SAO Incident in 2022. A prologue comic was distributed for free during the Dengeki Fall Festival of 2017 and was also released free of charge via the internet in October 2017.

How to PLAY this game?

When in doubt, level up. Sword Art Online: Integral Factor is loaded with opportunities to gain level-boosting EXP to help build your character up to par for the current floor in no time flat. All quests have suggested levels so you can start with the lower-ranked ones first, but if you're impatient, you can also try tackling some of the higher-ranked quests with the help of another player. It’s worth pointing out that some quests are also repeatable, most notably the monster-hunting subquests you'll find on each floor. Hit these early and often until you're appropriately swole. Beyond leveling up your character, Sword Art Online: Integral Factor also lets you equip Sword Skills to customize your hero's abilities. Weapons have four slots for Attack Skills (quick, high-damage abilities that have cooldowns) while armors have four slots for Buffs (passive, always-on bonuses). It behooves you to fill these as soon as you find relevant Sword Skills along your adventure.
